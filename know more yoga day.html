General Guidelines For Practicing Yoga


BEFORE THE PRACTICE

1.Yoga should be practiced in a calm and quiet atmosphere with a relaxed body and mind.
2.Yoga should be practised on an empty stomach or light stomach. Consume small amount of honey in lukewarm water if you feel weak.
3.It is advisable to use a mattress, Yoga mat, durrie or folded blanket for the practice.
4.Light and comfortable cotton clothes are preferred to facilitate easy movement of the body
5.In case of chronic disease/ pain/ cardiac problems/pregnancy, a physician or a Yoga therapist should be consulted prior to performing Yogic practices



DURING THE PRACTICE

1.Yogic practices shall be performed slowly, in a relaxed manner, with an awareness of the body and breath.
2.Breathing should be always through the nostrils unless instructed otherwise
3.Do not hold body tightly, or jerk the body at any point of time.
4.Perform the practices according to your own capacity.


AFTER THE PRACTICE

Bath should be taken only 20-30 minutes after practice.
Food may be consumed only after 20-30 minutes of practice.
